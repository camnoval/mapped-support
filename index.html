Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't my map showing locations?
The app needs photos with GPS data. Make sure location services were enabled when you took photos. Check Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Location Services ‚Üí Camera.
How does the app count "places visited"?
A "place" is defined as a cluster of photos within 5 miles of each other during the same time period. Once you move to a new location (more than 5 miles away), that counts as a new place. This prevents your local coffee shop from counting as 50 different places.
Is my data private?
Yes. All processing happens on your device. We never upload your photos or location data to any server. Delete the app and everything disappears.
Can I exclude certain photos?
Yes! Go to Settings within the app to deselect specific photos or date ranges. Your map updates automatically.
How accurate is the location data?
We use the exact GPS coordinates from your photos. Accuracy is typically 5-10 meters outdoors, less accurate indoors.
Is there a subscription?
No subscriptions, no hidden fees. One-time purchase.
